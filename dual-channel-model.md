## Introduction
We present some speech samples in the website to show the dual-channel magnitude-phase ;earning with cross-attention for bone-conducted speech. Objective and subjective evaluations on A4BS dataset show that our system substantially outperforms existing bone-conducted speech enhancement systems.

## Samples
We encourage our readers to listen to the following audio samples in order to experience the audio quality of our enhanced speech.
